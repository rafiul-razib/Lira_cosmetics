Lira Cosmetics is a premium beauty and personal care brand dedicated to delivering high-quality skincare, makeup, and self-care products. The brand focuses on enhancing natural beauty by combining modern cosmetic science with carefully selected ingredients. Lira Cosmetics offers a diverse range of products including facial creams, serums, cleansers, cosmetics, and personal care essentials, all designed to meet the needs of different skin types and preferences. The company aims to provide safe, effective, and affordable beauty solutions for everyday use.

Delivery Policy

Lira Cosmetics ensures a reliable and efficient delivery system across Bangladesh. Orders placed through official platforms are typically processed quickly and delivered within 3 to 5 business days, depending on the customer’s location. Delivery services operate nationwide to ensure accessibility for customers in both urban and rural areas. At this time, Lira Cosmetics does not offer international shipping, meaning orders can only be delivered within Bangladesh.

Return Policy

Customer satisfaction is a priority for Lira Cosmetics. Products purchased from the brand can be returned within 7 days of delivery, provided they remain unopened, unused, and in their original packaging. This policy ensures product safety and hygiene standards are maintained. Items that have been opened, used, or tampered with are not eligible for return or exchange. Customers are encouraged to review products carefully upon receiving them.

Payment Methods

Lira Cosmetics supports multiple convenient payment options to make purchasing easier for customers. Accepted payment methods include popular mobile financial services such as bKash, Nagad, and Rocket, as well as traditional credit and debit card payments. This flexibility allows customers to choose their preferred payment method securely and efficiently.

Customer Support

Lira Cosmetics provides dedicated customer support to assist users with inquiries, orders, and product-related concerns. The support team is available from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Saturday through Thursday. During these hours, customers can reach out for assistance regarding orders, returns, payments, or general product information. The company aims to ensure a smooth and responsive customer experience.
